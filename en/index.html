Welcome to runter.net

A small personal website for profiles, projects, hardware notes, and the things I am building.

What is this?

runter.net is my personal corner of the web. It collects information about me, the projects I am working on, and the technical setups I care about, from Minecraft mods to servers and PC hardware.

Explore

  • About has profiles and personal information.
  • Projects shows the things I am building or have built.
  • Runtergesprungen contains my profile, hardware, and current interests.

Development

The site is intentionally simple: fast pages, dark and light mode, and German/English versions. I use it as both a home page and a place to practice building clean static websites.
